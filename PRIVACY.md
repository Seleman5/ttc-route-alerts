# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6/15/2026

## TTC Route Alerts

TTC Route Alerts is a transit utility app that helps users save TTC routes, view route alerts, find nearby TTC stops, and check live arrival predictions.

## Data We Store

TTC Route Alerts stores saved routes and app preferences locally on the user’s device. This may include:

* saved TTC routes
* route nicknames
* notification preferences
* refresh preferences
* cached TTC alert data
* cached arrival preview data

This information is stored on the device and is not sent to our own server.

## Location Use

The app may request location permission to show nearby TTC stops and nearby live arrival predictions.

Location is used to:

* find nearby TTC stops
* calculate distance to stops
* show live arrival predictions for nearby stops and saved routes

The app does not use location for advertising, tracking, account identification, or selling user data.

## TTC Data

The app fetches TTC service information from public TTC data sources, including TTC alert feeds and TTC real-time arrival prediction services.

When checking live arrival predictions, the app may request arrival information for a selected TTC stop from TTC public services.

## Notifications

The app uses local notifications to alert users when a saved route has a matching TTC service alert.

Notifications are based on refreshed TTC alert data and saved routes. The app does not use remote push notifications at this time.

## Accounts

TTC Route Alerts does not require a user account.

## Analytics and Tracking

TTC Route Alerts does not include third-party advertising tracking or analytics tracking.

## Data Sharing

TTC Route Alerts does not sell user data.

The app does not share saved routes or location data with our own server. TTC public services may receive standard network requests needed to fetch transit information, such as alert feeds or stop prediction requests.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete saved routes inside the app. Users can also remove all locally stored app data by deleting the app from their device.

## Children’s Privacy

TTC Route Alerts is a general transit utility app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
